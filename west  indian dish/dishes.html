Delicious Dishes of West India

Puran Poli

Puran Poli is a sweet flatbread stuffed with a mixture of jaggery and split yellow gram. It is a traditional dish from Maharashtra, often enjoyed during festivals.

Vada Pav

Vada Pav is a popular street food from Mumbai, consisting of a spicy potato filling sandwiched between a bread roll, served with chutneys and fried green chili.

Goan Fish Curry

Goan Fish Curry is a tangy and spicy dish made with fish, coconut milk, and a blend of spices. It is a staple in Goan cuisine, often served with rice.

Dhokla

Dhokla is a savory steamed cake made from fermented rice and chickpea batter. It is a popular snack from Gujarat, often served with green chutney.

Bhelpuri

Bhelpuri is a popular Indian street food made with puffed rice, sev, vegetables, and a tangy tamarind sauce.

Shrikhand

Shrikhand is a traditional Indian dessert made with strained yogurt, sweetened with sugar and flavored with cardamom and saffron.
